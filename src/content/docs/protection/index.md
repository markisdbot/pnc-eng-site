# Protection & Control

GOOSE Works delivers expert protection and control engineering services for power systems. We combine deep technical knowledge with practical field experience to solve complex protection challenges.

## Services

### Protection Engineering
- Relay settings calculations and coordination studies
- Protection scheme design for transmission and distribution
- Distance, overcurrent, and differential protection
- Breaker failure and bus protection schemes

### Control Systems
- SCADA system design and integration
- RTU and PLC programming
- HMI development and alarm management
- Control panel design and wiring diagrams

### Commissioning & Testing
- Protection system commissioning
- End-to-end testing
- Relay testing and verification
- Documentation and turnover packages

## Experience

Our engineers have worked on protection and control projects ranging from distribution substations to extra-high-voltage transmission systems. We understand the critical nature of protection systems and the importance of getting it right the first time.

## Standards & Compliance

We design to industry standards including:
- IEEE C37 series (relay and control standards)
- IEC 61850 (substation automation)
- NERC PRC standards (protection system requirements)
- Utility-specific engineering standards
